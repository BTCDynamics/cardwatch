Why This Version?

iPhone Safari often blocks live camera streaming from a local HTTP address. This version uses the native iPhone camera picker, which works better on local CardDesk installs.

Best Results
  • Use a phone mount or tripod.
  • Use soft light from both sides.
  • Avoid direct flash and glare.
  • Use a dark matte background.
  • Fill the frame with one card.
  • For slabs, make sure the label is readable.
